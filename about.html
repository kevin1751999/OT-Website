About


Founder

Organoterra

  • Kevin Patel, founder of Organoterra, is a passionate entrepreneur with a deep understanding of agriculture.
  • Rooted in a family business steeped in agricultural machinery, Kevin's passion for sustainability was further ignited during his engineering studies at IIT Roorkee. There, he actively led projects focused on sustainable transformation and social change.
  • Combining his engineering expertise with his family legacy, Kevin identified a critical need for eco-friendly and affordable farm inputs in India. This led him to found Organoterra, a company dedicated to empowering farmers.
  • A dedicated leader, Kevin is building a team that shares his vision of revolutionizing Indian agriculture. Through Organoterra's innovative solutions, they strive to make sustainable practices accessible and profitable for farmers while promoting a healthier environment.

The Journey

As he travelled through rural India, exploring its agrarian heart, he was struck by the challenges faced by farmers. Disposing of agro-industrial residue posed a significant hurdle, and the lack of readily available organic farm inputs made the transition to sustainable practices difficult. Witnessing their struggle to grow organic food fueled his determination to find a solution.

This journey led him to an innovative approach: transforming the very waste they produced into potent fodder, organic fertilizers and pest repellents. Thus, Organoterra was born - a testament to the power of sustainable solutions and empowering farmers to cultivate a thriving future.
